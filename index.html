What ShieldHER Does

Every safety feature
you actually need

Built for real situations. Not just theoretical ones.

🆘

SOS Emergency Alert

Hold the SOS button for 5 seconds to instantly SMS all your trusted contacts with your exact GPS location and street address. Works even on silent mode.

🚶‍♀️

Safe Walk Timer

Start a countdown when walking alone. If you don't check in before the timer expires, your contacts are automatically alerted that something may be wrong.

✅

Check-In System

Schedule regular check-ins. Miss one and your trusted contacts get notified immediately. Peace of mind for you and the people who care about you.

📞

Fake Call Escape

Set up a fake incoming call to escape uncomfortable situations. Looks completely real — full ringing screen with a custom name and number.

🗺️

Danger Zone Map

Community-reported safety alerts on a live map. See what's happening in your area and contribute reports to help other women stay safe.

👥

Trusted Contacts

Add family and friends as trusted contacts. They receive SMS alerts with your real-time location whenever you need help — no app required on their end.

How ShieldHER works

1

Add Trusted Contacts

Add family and friends by phone number. They don't need to install anything — they just receive SMS alerts.

2

Trigger When Needed

Hold the SOS button, start a safe walk, or schedule a check-in. ShieldHER monitors your safety automatically.

3

Contacts Get Alerted

Your contacts receive an instant SMS with your name, GPS coordinates, and a Google Maps link to your exact location.

4

Help Arrives Faster

Your loved ones know exactly where you are and what's happening — so help reaches you faster when it matters most.
