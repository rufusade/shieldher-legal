Privacy Policy

ShieldHER — Your safety, always with you

ShieldHER is a women's personal safety application. We take your privacy seriously because your safety depends on it. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it. We will never sell your data to third parties.

📋

Data We Collect

Account Information

  • Full name and email address (provided at registration)
  • Password (stored securely via Firebase Authentication — we never see it)
  • Profile information you choose to add

Usage Data

  • SOS alerts you trigger (timestamp, status, location)
  • Safe walk sessions (duration, start/end time, location)
  • Check-in records (timestamp, message, status)
  • Community danger zone reports you submit

Device Data

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token for push notifications
  • Device type and operating system (collected automatically by Firebase)
📍

Location Data

Location is the core of ShieldHER's safety features. We collect your GPS coordinates only when safety features are active — never silently in the background without your knowledge.

When we collect location:

  • When you trigger an SOS alert (one-time snapshot)
  • During an active Safe Walk session (every 10 seconds)
  • When you send a manual check-in
  • When you report a danger zone

How location is used:

  • Included in emergency SMS alerts sent to your trusted contacts
  • Shown as a Google Maps link so contacts can find you
  • Stored in your safety history in Firestore
  • Never shared with advertisers or third parties

You can revoke location permissions at any time in your device settings. Some features will be limited without location access.

👥

Trusted Contacts

When you add a trusted contact, we store their name, phone number, and relationship label in your private Firestore account. This data is:

  • Only accessible by you and our backend functions
  • Used solely to send emergency SMS alerts on your behalf
  • Never shared with other users or third parties
  • Deleted when you remove the contact or delete your account

By adding someone as a trusted contact, you confirm you have their consent to receive emergency SMS notifications from ShieldHER on your behalf.

💬

SMS Notifications

ShieldHER uses Twilio to send SMS messages to your trusted contacts during emergencies. When an alert is triggered:

  • Your trusted contacts receive an SMS with your name and GPS location link
  • Messages are sent from a ShieldHER Twilio phone number
  • Your personal phone number is never revealed to contacts via SMS
  • Contacts can reply STOP to opt out of future messages

Twilio's privacy policy applies to SMS delivery. We share only the minimum data necessary (recipient phone number and message content) with Twilio.

🔒

Data Storage & Security

All data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore), which provides:

  • Encryption at rest and in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Role-based access controls
  • SOC 2 Type II compliance
  • Data centers in the United States

We implement Firestore security rules to ensure users can only access their own data. Our backend Cloud Functions access data only as needed to deliver emergency notifications.

Data Retention

  • Safety history (SOS, safe walks, check-ins) retained for 90 days by default
  • Account data retained until you delete your account
  • Danger zone reports retained until moderated or removed
⚖️

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your data at any time
  • Correction — update your name, email, or contact information in the app
  • Deletion — delete your account from the Profile screen; this removes your auth record and user data
  • Portability — request an export of your safety history
  • Withdrawal — revoke location or notification permissions at any time in device settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@shieldher.app.

🚸

Children's Privacy

ShieldHER is designed for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us immediately at support@shieldher.app and we will delete the account promptly.
